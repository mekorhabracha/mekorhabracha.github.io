Welcome to Mekor Habracha!

This is the online home of our thriving community in Center City Philadelphia. Stop by to know more about who we are, listen to Rabbi Hirsch's drashot, learn more about the resources that make Philadelphia a great place to be Jewish or sign up for our weekly newsletter.

Rabbi Eliezer Hirsch

Under Rabbi Hirsch's tenure Mekor Habracha has experienced exponential growth, leading the renaissance of the Jewish community in Center City, which now boasts a fully functioning eruv, a morning minyan in the Rittenhouse district, and a wide variety of kosher establishments.
