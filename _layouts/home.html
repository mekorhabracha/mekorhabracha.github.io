Welcome to Mekor Habracha!

This is the online home of our thriving Modern Orthodox community in Center City Philadelphia. Stop by to know more about who we are, listen to Rabbi Hirsch's drashot, learn more about the resources that make Philadelphia a great place to be Jewish or sign up for our weekly newsletter.

High Holidays at Mekor Habracha

The High Holidays are right around the corner! We are accepting limited reservations for services at Mekor on a first come, first served basis. You can make your reservation by emailing the shul.

All synagogue members can attend all High Holiday services free of charge, but they must reserve seats — reservations are not automatic. Other visitors can make their reservations at a price per person of $200 for all Rosh Hashanah and $200 for all of Yom Kippur. Nonmember students have access to tickets for either Rosh Hashanah or Yom Kippur services at a discounted price of $125 per person. Reservations are complete once payment is received, if applicable.

There will be children's programming available during services. Please contact the shul to sign up.

Best wishes for a happy and healthy new year!
