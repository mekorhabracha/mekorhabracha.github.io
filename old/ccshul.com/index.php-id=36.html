Coming up

Mekor Bike Club - 1st Ride Monday, July 4, after 9am Minyan
As an outgrowth of our impressive accomplishments in other athletic endeavors, Mekor Habracha is starting a bike club. To join, one needs a road bike and a helmet (which you must wear while riding), and be a member of the shul. If you are interested please email the club president, Bruce Taubman, at btaubman@comcast.net. We hope you will join - it should be a lot of fun!

NEW! Kashrut in Center City - Monday, July 11, 6:30-7:30 PM
Rabbi Hirsch will meet with the Mekor Habracha community to provide the latest kashrut news in Center City, and answer kosher questions from our members.  Guests from outside the Mekor community are welcome to attend.

NEW! Torah Mindfulness - Thursday, July 21, 6:30-7:30 PM
A Practical Jewish Text-Based Approach, taught by Rabbi Hirsch
This introductory class will provide a practical Jewish approach to mindfulness, based on Torah. RSVP required; please email mekorhabracha@gmail.com. $10 suggested donation to the shul. Everyone is welcome to attend.

Save the Date Sunday, September 18 -- Join us for Mekor Habracha’s 2nd Annual Gala, honoring Hersh Waisbord and Elana & Danny Schwartz. Exciting details to follow! For any questions or if you would like to join the Gala Committee, please email Joel Frisch at jafrisch@gmail.com.

Exciting Learning Opportunities!! Everyone in the community is welcome to attend:
Shabbat Afternoon Talmud Class, led by Rabbi Hirsch, begins 45 minutes prior to Mincha.
Morning learning group/chabura is held Monday-Friday and begins immediately following Shacharit. If you are interested in participating, please contact the shul or the rabbi.
Talmud Class, led by Adam Romanoski, is now resuming on Tuesdays at 7pm.

Newly Kosher
We are pleased to announce that The Tasty, a vegan diner in South Philadelphia, is now certified Kosher under the Orthodox supervision of the IKC!  The Tasty is located at 1401 S.12th Street (12th & Reed), on Passyunk Square.  Please support our efforts by informing them that you are a kosher consumer!
