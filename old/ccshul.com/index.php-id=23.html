The High Holidays are right around the corner! Limited reservations for services at Mekor Habracha will be accepted on a first come, first served basis. Synagogue members who make a reservation attend all High Holiday services free of charge.

Cost of membership is: Singles: $375, Couples/Families: $700, Students: $200.

Membership forms can be found here: 

*Note: If you joined after January 2015 and would like to have your renewal for the coming year prorated, please let us know.

In addition to attending High Holiday services free of charge, members now have a vote in certain shul elections, will receive discounts throughout the year on shul related offerings such as community meals and programs, kiddush and other sponsorships, and use of synagogue space for special occasions.

IMPORTANT: Members MUST reserve seats by emailing the shul at mekorhabracha@gmail.com. Reservations will NOT be automatically made for members.

Non-Member seating cost: $200 per person (students $125) for all of Rosh Hashana and $200 per person (students $125) for all of Yom Kippur.

Reserve by emailing mekorhabracha@gmail.com Reservations are not complete until payment is received. Payment information can be found here: Donation Info

We are happy to assist anyone needing flexible payment schedules or cost adjustments. Please email mekorhabracha@gmail.com for any such requests.

Children's programming available - please contact the shul to sign up.

Questions? Send all inquiries here: mekorhabracha@gmail.com

Best wishes for a happy and healthy new year!
