Shavuot at Mekor Habracha

You’re invited to celebrate the holiday of Shavuot with the Center City Jewish community! Enjoy delicious dairy meals and snacks, learn from leading local rabbis and educators, and be part of the biggest Shavuot event Center City has ever seen. The evening will kick off with keynote speaker Elsie Stern, Ph.D., followed by sessions throughout the night. Stay for some or all of the evening, concluding with sunrise services. Childcare available upon request. All are welcome. 

Saturday, May 23, 8:00 pm-Sunday, May 24 at 7:00 am
The Gershman Y at Broad and Pine
Register at phillyshavuot.com

Suggested donation $10-25 (students $5-18)
Sign up to volunteer!

Sponsored by the Center City Kehillah, a network of communities representing the diversity of Jewish life in urban Philadelphia, in cooperation with and funded by the Jewish Federation of Greater Philadelphia. Shavuot Program

Join the Mekor Habracha community for our ninth annual Shavuot all-night learning program! On Tuesday, June 3, evening services begin at 8:15 PM, followed by Shavuot dinner at 9:00 PM (by reservation only), and learning program at 10:45 PM featuring great classes from members of the Mekor Habracha community, as well as refreshments and lots of caffeine. Morning sunrise services begin at 5:00 AM. Everyone is welcome to attend.
 
BREAKING NEWS: Mekor Habracha WILL be having a 9:15 AM minyan on Wednesday morning, June 4 (the first day of Shavuot), in addition to the sunrise morning minyan. Thank you to everyone who helped put this together!
